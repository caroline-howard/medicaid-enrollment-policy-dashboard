# Source Notes

Source notes will be completed after public Medicaid/CHIP data sources are selected and validated.

## Candidate Source Categories

- Data.Medicaid.gov Medicaid and CHIP open data: https://data.medicaid.gov/
- State Medicaid and CHIP Applications, Eligibility Determinations, and Enrollment Data: https://data.medicaid.gov/dataset/6165f45b-ca93-5bb5-9d06-db29c692a360/data
- Medicaid and CHIP Eligibility Operations and Enrollment Snapshot Data: https://data.medicaid.gov/medicaid-chip-eligibility-enrollment-snapshot-data
- Medicaid.gov Medicaid and CHIP Eligibility Operations and Enrollment Snapshot: https://www.medicaid.gov/medicaid-and-chip-eligibility-operations-and-enrollment-snapshot
- Medicaid.gov Medicaid and CHIP Enrollment Data: https://www.medicaid.gov/medicaid/national-medicaid-chip-program-information/medicaid-chip-enrollment-data
- State Medicaid agency documentation
- Supporting policy context from public, cited sources

## Source Validation Checklist

- Confirm publisher and URL.
- Confirm reporting period.
- Confirm update date.
- Confirm field definitions.
- Confirm whether values are preliminary, revised, or final.
- Document missingness and reporting caveats.
